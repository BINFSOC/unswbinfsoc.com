Our Mission

At BINFSOC, our mission is to unite students from bioinformatics as well as computer science, statistics, biology and chemistry disciplines, to create a network of future thinkers and innovators.


We aim to equip students with fundamental skills they need to excel in industry, whilst enriching the student life experience.
